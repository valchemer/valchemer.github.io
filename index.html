Project: How to Circle Forward in Time?

This work explores non-linear approaches to architectural preservation using Tempelhof Airport in Berlin as a case study. Through AI-generated imagery, theoretical mappings, and temporal diagrams, it questions the dominance of linear historiography and suggests alternate readings of the city through memory, ruination, and reconstruction.
